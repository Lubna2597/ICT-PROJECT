"Maria loved spending her afternoons in the old city library. It was quiet, warm, and filled with the smell of old books. Every day after school, she sat near the large window where sunlight fell gently across the wooden table.One day, as she was returning a book, she noticed a small, dusty journal tucked behind a row of thick history books. Curious, she opened it. The pages were filled with tiny handwriting and sketches of places she had never seen before—mountains, old streets, and strange symbols.At the end of the journal, she found a note: “To whoever finds this, the map inside leads to something precious, but only the patient will discover it.” Maria felt a rush of excitement. She wasn't sure what the “precious” thing was, but she knew one thing—she wanted to find out."
